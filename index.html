🗺 Skovde: Visualised

A Week of Challenges and Discovery

A group of students from Howest spent a week in Skovde, Sweden, competing in a Capture The Flag competition alongside students from Skovde University. The challenges ranged from cryptography to real-world scavenger hunts across the campus, the evenings brought board games, pub nights, and a city that had a habit of going quiet by ten on weekdays. This is how the week went, day by day, from the perspective of our group.
