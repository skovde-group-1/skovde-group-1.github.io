Monday — Project Kickoff

On Monday, the project officially kicked off. We started the day with a small introduction where everyone had the chance to get to know each other better. After that, we met our teams and started working on the project until around 4 PM. At noon, we went out for sushi together with the local students, which was a great way to connect and spend time together outside of the project work. In the evening, we had our first social activity: a board game night. Before and after the games, we spent time talking, having drinks, and getting to know everyone better. It was a fun and relaxed way to end the first day of the project.
