Our International Project in Skövde

One week of learning, culture, teamwork, and experiences in Sweden 🇸🇪

Saturday — Arrival Day

Today we woke up at 3:00 AM to catch our flight to Sweden. After a long journey, we finally arrived in Göteborg (where we had to wait for 3 hours because they forgot to put out luggage on the plane) and then took a train to Skövde. At approximately 5:30 PM, we arrived at our accommodation and went to the ICA Max for grocery shopping. We ended up being in this store for about 2 hours, which was quite an adventure! After that, we attempted to make dinner, but it turned out to be a bit of a disaster. We still ate it, but it was definitely not our best culinary creation. We had to improvise with the ingredients we had, and it was definitely an experience we won't forget! After dinner we went had a few drinks and went to bed early to rest for the exciting week ahead.

Most memorable moment: Our first meal in Skövde

Sunday — Getting to Know Everyone

Today we had time to settle in and get to know the other participants. We explored local places, shared cultures, and discussed the upcoming project activities.

What I learned: Different cultures bring different perspectives.

Monday — Project Kickoff

The official project started today. We were introduced to the objectives, formed teams, and began working on our tasks.

Main activity: Team formation and brainstorming.

Tuesday — Teamwork & Workshops

We participated in workshops and continued collaborating on the project. Communication and teamwork became very important today.

Challenge: Solving problems together in an international team.
