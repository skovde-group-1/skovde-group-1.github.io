Overview

We traveled with a group of 25 cybersecurity students to Skövde, Sweden, to compete in an engaging and fun CTF week. We teamed up with Skövde University students to compete against each other and see who was the best at CTF problem solving. Along the way, we made a lot of new friends, created great memories, and overall had an amazing project week.

A Week of Challenges and Discovery

A group of students from Howest spent a week in Skövde, Sweden, competing in a Capture The Flag competition alongside students from Skövde University. The challenges ranged from cryptography to real-world scavenger hunts across the campus; the evenings brought board games, pub nights, and a city that had a habit of going quiet by ten on weekdays. This is how the week went, day by day, from the perspective of our group.
